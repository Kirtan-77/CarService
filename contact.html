Common Questions

FAQ

How do I book a service?

You can book online using the form above, call us directly, or walk in to our service center. Online bookings are confirmed within 2 hours.

Do you provide doorstep pickup?

Yes! We offer free doorstep pickup and drop for vehicles within 10 km of our service center for bookings above ₹1,500.

How long does a service take?

It depends on the service. A basic oil change takes 45 minutes, while complex repairs like engine overhaul may take 1–3 days. We'll give you a time estimate upfront.

Do you use original spare parts?

Absolutely. We only use genuine, OEM-grade spare parts. If an alternative part is used, we always inform the customer and get approval first.

Is there a service warranty?

Yes! All our services come with a warranty ranging from 1 month to 1 year depending on the type of work done. If an issue recurs, we fix it free of charge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and net banking. No advance payment required for basic services.
