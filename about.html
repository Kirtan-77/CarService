Our Story

BUILT ON TRUST,
DRIVEN BY PASSION

CarService was founded in 2010 by a team of passionate automotive engineers who believed that vehicle servicing should be transparent, affordable, and reliable — not a frustrating experience for car owners.

What started as a small garage with 3 mechanics has grown into a full-service automotive center with over 50 certified professionals, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Mumbai.

"We don't just fix cars — we build relationships. Every customer who walks in is treated like family, and every vehicle is treated like our own."

Today, CarService is one of the most trusted names in vehicle maintenance — known for honest pricing, fast turnaround, and work that lasts.

Our Journey

HOW WE GREW

From a small garage to a trusted service center — here's our story in milestones.

2010

The Beginning

CarService opened its doors with 3 mechanics, a small workshop in Mumbai, and a big dream — to make honest car servicing accessible to everyone.

2014

Expanding the Team

Grew to 15 mechanics and added dedicated bays for AC service, denting & painting, and electrical repairs. Crossed 1,000 satisfied customers.

2018

Going Digital

Launched our online booking system and customer portal. Introduced computerized diagnostics and OBD scanning for faster, accurate repairs.

2022

EV & Hybrid Ready

Trained our entire team on Electric Vehicle servicing. Installed EV diagnostic tools and became one of Mumbai's few EV-certified service centers.

2024

12,000+ Cars & Counting

Today, with 50+ experts and a 98% satisfaction rate, CarService continues to set the standard for transparent, reliable vehicle care in the city.
